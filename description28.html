Love-Love

Add a sporty twist to your date night with "Love-Love." Grab your tennis rackets and head over to the tennis courts for an exhilarating game. Challenge each other in at least one set of 7 matches. To sweeten the deal, the loser gets to choose and pay for the post-tennis drinks.

How it Works:

  1. Tennis Showdown: Bring out your competitive spirit on the tennis court. Engage in at least one set of 7 matches, showcasing your tennis skills and enjoying the physical activity together.
  2. Post-Game Refreshment: After the tennis showdown, head to a nearby spot for post-game drinks. The twist: the loser of the match gets to choose the venue and foot the bill for the refreshments.

Tips for Maximum Fun:

  • Keep the tennis match lighthearted and fun. It's about enjoying the game and spending quality time together, not just about winning.
  • Regardless of who wins or loses, celebrate the effort put into the game. Acknowledge each other's skills and improvements, fostering a supportive atmosphere.!
  • Bring water to stay hydrated during the game, especially if you're playing in warmer weather. Keeping energy levels up enhances the overall experience.
  • After the tennis match and drinks, take a moment to reflect on the fun and laughter shared during the Love-Love date. It's a great opportunity to appreciate the joys of being active together.
