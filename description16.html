Always Room for Ice Cream

Take a leisurely stroll to your nearest gelato supplier, savouring the anticipation of a frozen treat. Along the way, engage in friendly competition by taking turns picking themes and naming as much as possible in a category. The loser gets the sweet privilege of treating the winner to delicious ice cream.

How it Works:

  1. Sweet Stroll: Enjoy a leisurely walk to your nearest gelato supplier, relishing the anticipation of your frozen dessert.
  2. Category Countdown: Take turns picking categories (e.g., favorite movies, animals, or places) and name as many items as possible within the chosen theme.
  3. Friendly Competition: Engage in a friendly competition, challenging each other to think quickly and creatively within the selected categories.
  4. Ice Cream Reward: The loser of each round treats the winner to a delectable serving of gelato, turning the competition into a sweet indulgence.
  5. Savour the Victory: Celebrate the winner's triumph with a delightful ice cream treat, enjoying the sweet taste of victory together.

Tips for Maximum Fun:

  • Choose a variety of categories to keep the game engaging and explore each other's knowledge and preferences.
  • Use the stroll to engage in casual conversations, deepening your connection as you discuss the categories and your favorite items.
  • Introduce playful bets or challenges for added excitement, making each round of the game more dynamic.
  • Experiment with different gelato flavors, making the ice cream treat even more exciting and adventurous.
  • Document your ice cream date with photos, creating memories of the sweet moments shared during your delightful walk.
